The Beginning

Born from a Passion for Authentic Style

Founded in 2020 by John Finlo, Velvet Vogue emerged from a simple but powerful idea: fashion should empower, not intimidate. Starting with a small curated edit of pieces that blended comfort with elegance, the brand quickly resonated with a generation seeking clothes that authentically expressed who they are.

From our base in Colombo, Sri Lanka, we source from ethical suppliers across Asia and Europe, working closely with artisans who share our commitment to quality and craft. Every garment that carries the Velvet Vogue name has been chosen with intention.

Today, Velvet Vogue ships to over 40 countries, but our ethos remains unchanged: clothes that make you feel extraordinary, every single day.
